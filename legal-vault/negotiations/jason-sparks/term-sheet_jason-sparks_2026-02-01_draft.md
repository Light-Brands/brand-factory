## Conversion Terms

**Fixed Conversion Valuation:** $10,000,000 USD (pre-money)

**Equity on Conversion:** Approximately 1% of the Company's fully-diluted capitalization

**Conversion Mechanics:** At maturity, the Investor may elect to convert the outstanding principal into equity at the Fixed Conversion Valuation, receiving shares representing approximately 1% ownership of the Company.

---

## Repayment Option

**Investor Election:** At maturity, the Investor shall have the right, at their sole discretion, to elect one of the following:

### Option A — Conversion to Equity

Convert the full principal amount into equity at the Fixed Conversion Valuation of $10,000,000, representing approximately 1% ownership.

### Option B — Cash Repayment with Premium

Receive repayment of 1.2x the principal amount ($120,000 USD) in cash.

### Election Period

The Investor must provide written notice of their election no later than fifteen (15) days prior to the Maturity Date. If no election is made, the Note shall automatically convert to equity per Option A.

---

## Additional Terms

**Interest Rate:** 0% (the repayment premium serves in lieu of interest)

**Use of Funds:** General working capital and team operations

**Prepayment:** The Company may not prepay the Note without the Investor's written consent

**Pro Rata Rights:** The Investor shall have the right to participate in future equity financings on a pro-rata basis to maintain their ownership percentage

**Information Rights:** The Company shall provide quarterly updates on business progress and financial position
