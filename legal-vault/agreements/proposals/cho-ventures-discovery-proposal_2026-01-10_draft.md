The Consultant shall conduct up to six (6) structured discovery sessions totaling approximately 9-12 hours, covering:

| **Session 1** | Vision Alignment & Ecosystem Overview | Tony Cho, Leadership Team |

| **Session 4** | Authority Platform (Book, Course, Speaking) | Tony Cho, Content Team |
| **Session 5** | Technology Audit & Integration Requirements | IT/Operations |
| **Session 6** | Roadmap Presentation & Decision | All Stakeholders |

Prior to formal sessions, Consultant shall provide:
- Comprehensive pre-discovery questionnaires for all entities
- Current state documentation templates
- Stakeholder interview guides

## 3. Deliverables

Upon completion of the Discovery phase, Client shall receive the following deliverables:

### 3.1 Ecosystem Audit Report
A complete current state assessment of all Cho Ventures entities including:
- Metro 1 Commercial
- Future of Cities
- PHX-JAX District
- Climate + Innovation HUB
- ChoZen Retreat
- Cho Family Foundation
- M1 Community Fund

### 3.2 AI Opportunity Matrix
A prioritized matrix identifying AI integration opportunities across all entities, ranked by:
- Implementation complexity
- Business impact potential
- Resource requirements
- Dependencies

### 3.3 Unified Architecture Design
A comprehensive system blueprint illustrating:
- Central CRM hub structure
- Entity integration points
- Data flow diagrams
- Technology stack recommendations

### 3.4 Community Platform Specification
Detailed requirements for the ChoZen community app including:
- Feature specifications
- User journey maps
- Technical architecture
- Integration requirements

### 3.5 Authority Platform Plan
Strategic roadmap for thought leadership assets:
- Book launch strategy
- Online course architecture
- Speaking and media engine
- Newsletter and content systems

### 3.6 Implementation Roadmap
A phased implementation plan including:
- Phase 2 (Implementation) detailed breakdown
- Phase 3 (Authority Launch) detailed breakdown
- Dependencies and prerequisites
- Recommended sequencing

### 3.7 Investment Framework
Complete financial analysis including:
- Component-level pricing for future phases
- Monthly operating cost projections
- ROI projections and success metrics
- Payment milestone recommendations

### 3.8 Final Presentation
A comprehensive presentation of all findings and recommendations delivered to Client stakeholders.
