## PART VIII: RISK MANAGEMENT & CONTINGENCIES

### 8.1 Risk Matrix

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Slow pipeline** | Medium | High | Double content output, expand channels |
| **Delivery quality issues** | Low | High | Quality gates, client check-ins, templates |
| **Founder burnout** | High | Critical | Hire ops support early, batch work, boundaries |
| **Cash flow gaps** | Medium | High | 50% upfront payment, conservative spend |
| **Competitor response** | Low | Medium | Speed advantage, community moat, open-source |
| **AI tool disruption** | Medium | Medium | Model agnostic, methodology > technology |

### 8.2 Contingency Playbooks

**If pipeline is slow (< 5 leads/week by Month 2)**:
1. Increase outreach volume 2x
2. Add new channel (partnerships, events)
3. Lower pricing temporarily for case studies
4. Personal network blitz (founder asks)

**If delivery takes too long (> 72 hours)**:
1. Audit time spent on each phase
2. Increase template usage
3. Add AI automation for research
4. Consider scope reduction

**If NPS drops below 60**:
1. Implement mid-engagement check-in
2. Review recent feedback for patterns
3. Add client success touchpoint
4. Refine scope-setting process

## PART IX: THE EXECUTION COMMANDMENTS

### Operating Principles

1. **Ship Weekly**: Every week produces visible progress. No invisible work.

2. **Measure Daily**: If it matters, it's tracked. Check dashboards every morning.

3. **Speed Over Perfection**: 80% shipped beats 100% planned. Iterate in public.

4. **Systemize Everything**: If you do it twice, create a template. If you do it ten times, automate it.

5. **Client Obsession**: Every decision passes the test: "Does this make clients more successful?"

6. **Compound Actions Only**: Every action should have >1x return. Content creates leads AND case studies. Engagements create revenue AND learning.

7. **Anti-Fragile Design**: Setbacks should make the system stronger. Document failures, update processes, improve.

8. **Transparent Defaults**: When in doubt, share more. Open metrics, open methodology, open learnings.

9. **Energy Management**: Protect the energy of the team. Sustainable pace > heroic sprints.

10. **Remember the Mission**: We're not building a consulting firm. We're building the future of how strategy gets done.
