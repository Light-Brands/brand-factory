**From Founders:**
- Transparent financials—everyone sees the numbers
- Salary increases tied to clear, achievable milestones
- Equity that creates real wealth upside
- No outside funding means no dilution, no board, no loss of control

**From Team:**
- Accept below-market compensation early
- Trust the plan and milestones
- Contribute to growth, not just delivery
- Stay through the journey (vesting)

**The Promise:**
In 18-24 months, every person on this team will earn $100K/year with equity worth 10x that in a company we built together, without giving away a single share to outside investors.
