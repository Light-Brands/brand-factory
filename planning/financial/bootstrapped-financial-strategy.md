# Bootstrapped Financial Strategy: Zero to $800K Payroll

## The Reality Check

**The Math We Must Solve:**
```
Target Annual Salaries:     $800,000 (8 people × $100K)
Employer Costs (~25%):      $200,000 (benefits, taxes, insurance)
Total People Cost:          $1,000,000/year
Monthly Burn for Team:      $83,333/month

Plus Operating Costs:       $150,000/year
Total Annual Requirement:   $1,150,000/year
Monthly Cash Requirement:   $95,833/month
```

**What This Means:**
To pay everyone $100K with zero outside funding, we need to generate approximately **$1.5M in revenue** annually (accounting for ~77% operating margin after scale costs).

---

## The Bootstrapped Flywheel Model

### Core Insight: Founders Eat Last, Then Feast

The mistake most bootstrapped companies make: trying to pay everyone market rate from day one. The winning strategy: **graduated compensation tied to real revenue milestones**.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    THE FLYWHEEL EQUATION                        │
│                                                                 │
│   Revenue → Reinvestment → Capacity → More Revenue → Salaries  │
│      ↑                                                    │    │
│      └────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘    │
│                                                                 │
│   Phase 1: Prove (2-3 founders, sweat equity)                  │
│   Phase 2: Validate (4 people, partial salaries)               │
│   Phase 3: Scale (6 people, $75K avg)                          │
│   Phase 4: Thrive (8 people, $100K target)                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Phase 0: The Anchor Partnership Phase (Month 1-2)

### Strategic Setup with Cho Ventures & Influex

These two partnerships are your **seed capital equivalent**—but better, because they're revenue + case studies + referrals.

#### The "Charter Partner" Structure

**What You Offer:**
- Discounted rate (40-50% off standard pricing)
- Priority access and white-glove service
- Extended engagement scope
- Co-creation of methodology (they shape the tools)

**What You Get:**
- Guaranteed revenue to fund initial operations
- Permission to use as named case studies
- Warm introductions to 3-5 similar companies
- Testimonials and social proof
- Real-world validation of methodology

**What You PROTECT:**
- All frameworks, templates, and tools remain your IP
- Case study rights with approval process
- Methodology learnings feed your platform
- Data and insights aggregate to your advantage

### Cho Ventures Engagement Structure

**Profile:** VC firm needing portfolio-wide brand/positioning support

**Proposed Charter Partnership:**

| Element | Standard | Charter Partner Rate |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
