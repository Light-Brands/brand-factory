| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue | $100,000 |
| AI Costs | $500 |
| Strategist Time | 60 hours |
| Effective Hourly Rate | $1,658/hr |
| Gross Margin | 99% |

#### Partnership ($250K average)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue | $250,000 |
| AI Costs | $2,000 |
| Strategist Time | 200 hours |
| Effective Hourly Rate | $1,240/hr |
| Gross Margin | 99% |

**Insight**: The speed-of-thought model creates extraordinary unit economics because AI handles research/synthesis that would otherwise require junior consultants.
