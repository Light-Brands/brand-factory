## Channel Research Matrix

### Tier 1: Relationship Channels (Highest Conversion)

#### 1. VC/Accelerator Partnerships

**Hypothesis**: VCs and accelerators are trusted advisors to exactly our ICP. One partnership could yield 5-10 clients.

**Research Questions**:
- Which VCs/accelerators focus on AI-curious companies?
- What's their current AI consulting recommendation?
- What would make them refer to us?
- What's the partnership model (fee share, portfolio access, etc.)?

**Research Tasks**:
| Task | Method | Output |
|------|--------|--------|
| Map top 50 relevant VCs | LinkedIn + Crunchbase research | Tiered list with contact strategy |
| Identify accelerators with AI track | Web research | List with program timing |
| Interview 5 VC partners | Direct outreach | Partnership opportunity validation |
| Interview 3 accelerator leads | Direct outreach | Program integration opportunities |

**Top Targets** (to research and validate):
- a]6z Crypto
- Sequoia Scout network
- Y Combinator alumni network
- Techstars AI programs
- Antler
- On Deck

### Tier 2: Content Channels (Scale + Authority)

#### 4. Thought Leadership Content

**Hypothesis**: Content that demonstrates our capability sells better than content that describes it.

**Research Questions**:
- What content formats perform best for B2B consulting?
- What topics have high search volume + low competition?
- Where do our ICP consume content?

**Research Tasks**:
| Task | Method | Output |
|------|--------|--------|
| Content audit of top AI consultants | Web research | Content strategy gaps |
| Keyword research for AI consulting | SEMrush/Ahrefs analysis | SEO priority keywords |
| Interview ICP on content consumption | Client interviews | Content format priority |
| Analyze top LinkedIn posts in space | Manual analysis | Content playbook |

**Content Formats to Test**:
- LinkedIn long-form posts (free, high reach)
- Substack/newsletter (owned audience)
- YouTube/Loom case study videos (trust building)
- Twitter threads (viral potential)
- Podcast appearances (borrowed audience)

**Content Pillars**:
1. "Speed-of-thought" case studies (proof)
2. AI strategy frameworks (utility)
3. Industry-specific AI guides (targeting)
4. Behind-the-scenes methodology (differentiation)
5. Contrarian takes on AI hype (attention)

### Tier 3: Outbound Channels (Control + Speed)

#### 7. LinkedIn Outbound

**Hypothesis**: Personalized, value-first outreach to warm profiles can generate 1-2 meetings per week.

**Research Questions**:
- What's the optimal message sequence?
- What content warms up cold prospects?
- What targeting criteria work best?

**Research Tasks**:
| Task | Method | Output |
|------|--------|--------|
| Analyze top LinkedIn outreach examples | Swipe file collection | Message templates |
| Test 3 message sequences | A/B testing | Winning sequence |
| Build targeting criteria | ICP + LinkedIn filters | Lead list parameters |
| Measure response rates | Tracking | Optimization playbook |

**Outreach Sequence**:
1. Connect (personalized note referencing content/mutual)
2. Value (share relevant insight, no ask)
3. Engage (comment on their content)
4. Offer (invite to conversation, not pitch)

---

#### 8. Email Outbound

**Hypothesis**: Email to warm leads (content engagers, event attendees) converts at 5-10%.

**Research Questions**:
- What's the optimal email sequence?
- What subject lines perform?
- What's the right follow-up cadence?

**Research Tasks**:
| Task | Method | Output |
|------|--------|--------|
| Study B2B email best practices | Industry research | Email playbook |
| Build email templates | Copywriting | Template library |
| Set up email infrastructure | Tool setup | Outbound system |
| Test and optimize | A/B testing | Winning sequences |

**Research Tasks**:

- Slack/Discord group (always-on)

- Virtual mastermind (structured)
- Cohort-based learning (educational)
