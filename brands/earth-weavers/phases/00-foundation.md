## Purpose

## Founding Circle

### Composition Target
The founding circle should include 5-7 partners representing:

| Role | Purpose | Candidate Profile |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Visionary Lead | Overall vision holding | Original concept creators |
| Cultural Partner | Indigenous/cultural wisdom | Organization with indigenous leadership |
| Impact Partner | Development/environmental expertise | NGO with regenerative track record |
| Financial Partner | Funding and economic design | Impact investor or foundation |
| Media Partner | Communication and storytelling | Values-aligned media organization |
| Systems Partner | Governance and operations | Organization with scaling experience |
| Youth Partner | Next generation voice | Youth-led organization |

### Founding Circle Operating Agreements
- Meet monthly minimum during Phase 0
- Consensus decision-making on major choices
- Each partner contributes expertise, not just advice
- Shared risk and shared credit
- Exit process defined if partnership not working
