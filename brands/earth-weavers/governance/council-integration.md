### What the Council Does

- **Provides sacred alignment** ‚Äî Ensures Earth Weavers stays true to deeper purpose
- **Reviews major transitions** ‚Äî Gates, phase changes, mission-affecting decisions
- **Offers wisdom on request** ‚Äî Available for consultation on difficult questions

- **Make operational decisions** ‚Äî Daily operations are Brand Steward's domain

- **Override IAC on culture** ‚Äî IAC has autonomous authority on cultural matters

- **Intervene without invitation** ‚Äî Respects operational autonomy unless asked

## Gate Review Process

When a gate is ready for review:

1. **Gate Owner** completes all deliverables and marks as üü° Ready
2. **Brand Steward** confirms readiness
3. **Council Liaison** schedules Council review
4. **Council Member** (gate owner) leads review
5. **Council** deliberates and asks questions
6. **Decision:** Approved (üü¢), Needs Work (üü°), or Concerns (üî¥)
7. **If approved:** Gate is complete
8. **If needs work:** Specific remediation assigned
9. **All gates complete:** Full Council convenes for Phase approval

---

## Phase Transition Process

When a phase is complete:

1. **Brand Steward** presents completion evidence
2. **Founding Circle** confirms readiness to proceed
3. **Council Liaison** facilitates Council review
4. **Each Council Member** assesses from their perspective
5. **Council** provides consolidated guidance
6. **Decision:** Proceed, Proceed with conditions, or Pause
7. **If proceed:** Next phase begins
8. **If conditions:** Address before full proceed
9. **If pause:** Significant issues to address

## Requesting Council Guidance

### How to Request

1. **Identify the question** ‚Äî Be specific about what guidance is needed
2. **Provide context** ‚Äî Share relevant background
3. **Specify which Council member(s)** ‚Äî Which perspectives most needed?
4. **Note urgency** ‚Äî How soon is guidance needed?
5. **Submit through Council Liaison**

### Request Template

```

**From:** [Name/Role]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Urgency:** [High/Medium/Low]

### Question
[Specific question for Council]

### Context
[Relevant background and considerations]

### Council Members Most Relevant
- [Member 1]: [Why]
- [Member 2]: [Why]

### What We've Considered
[Options already explored]

### Decision Timeline

```

### Response Process

1. Council Liaison reviews request
2. Routes to relevant Council member(s)
3. Council member provides guidance
4. Liaison communicates back to requester
5. Guidance is advisory unless decision-requiring

## Council Operating Principles

- **Guides, doesn't command** ‚Äî Offers wisdom, not orders
- **Asks questions, doesn't just answer** ‚Äî Prompts reflection
- **Holds paradox** ‚Äî Multiple truths can coexist
- **Takes the long view** ‚Äî Generational perspective

### Working with Council Guidance

When Council provides guidance:

1. **Receive with openness** ‚Äî Even if challenging
2. **Reflect before reacting** ‚Äî Sit with it
3. **Ask clarifying questions** ‚Äî If unclear
4. **Integrate appropriately** ‚Äî Apply to context
5. **Report back** ‚Äî Close the loop on how guidance was used

### When Council Guidance Feels Difficult

1. **Don't dismiss** ‚Äî Council sees patterns we may miss
2. **Explore the discomfort** ‚Äî What's being triggered?
3. **Ask for elaboration** ‚Äî Seek to understand
4. **Find the integration** ‚Äî How can guidance inform action?
5. **If truly misaligned** ‚Äî Discuss openly with Council
