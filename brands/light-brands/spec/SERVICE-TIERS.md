## Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Approach

Light Brands pricing reflects:

1. **Transformation Value**: What is the brand worth to the client's success?
2. **Complexity**: How many moving parts and integrations?
3. **Timeline**: Standard vs. accelerated delivery
4. **Scope**: Breadth of deliverables and services
5. **Relationship**: New client vs. returning partner

### Investment Protection

All engagements include:
- Clear scope documentation
- Milestone-based payments
- Revision rounds built into pricing
- Quality guarantee
- Support window post-delivery

### Payment Terms

| Tier | Payment Structure |
|------|-------------------|
| Spark | 100% upfront |
| Ignite | 50% upfront, 50% on completion |
| Illuminate | 33% upfront, 33% midpoint, 34% completion |
| Radiate | Monthly or milestone-based |
| Transcend | Monthly retainer or custom |
