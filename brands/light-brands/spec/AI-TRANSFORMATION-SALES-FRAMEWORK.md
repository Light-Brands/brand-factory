# AI Transformation Sales Framework

## The Path from Discovery to Full Business Transformation

---

## Executive Summary

This framework defines Light Brands' approach to selling AI-powered business transformation services. The process is designed to meet clients where they are—starting with a low-barrier $500 discovery session and providing a clear, predictable path to comprehensive AI integration at the foundational level.

**Core Philosophy:** Replace broken systems and processes with modern, AI-empowered solutions that create lasting competitive advantage.

### Discovery Session Value Proposition

**Why $500?**
- Demonstrates client commitment (filters tire-kickers)

- Creates reciprocity—they've invested, we've delivered

**Conversion Target:** 60-70% proceed to Phase 2

## Objection Handling

| **"Can't you just tell me the price?"** | "I can give you ranges: Small businesses typically invest $40K-$90K for full transformation, Medium $120K-$275K. But the right investment depends on your specific situation—that's what discovery reveals." |
| **"That's expensive."** | "What's the cost of NOT transforming? Most clients find broken systems cost them 20-40% in efficiency. A $70K investment that saves $200K annually pays for itself in 4 months." |
| **"We want to DIY with AI tools."** | "Many try that path. The challenge isn't the tools—it's the strategy, integration, and change management. We've seen DIY attempts cost 3x more and take 5x longer. Discovery can show you where DIY works and where you need expertise." |
| **"We need to think about it."** | "Absolutely. What specific questions do you need answered? I want to make sure you have everything you need to make a confident decision." |
| **"Can we start smaller?"** | "That's exactly what the $500 discovery is for. It's the smallest commitment with the most clarity. From there, you'll know exactly what you need and can choose the right level." |
