## Pricing Philosophy: Value Over Time

### The Problem with Time-Based Pricing

Billing by the hour for this type of work creates misaligned incentives:
- Faster work = less revenue (punishes efficiency)
- Complex thinking = "just a few hours" (undervalues strategy)
- Platform development = variable time (unpredictable costs)
- AI-augmented delivery = exponentially faster (should command premium, not discount)

### The Value-Based Alternative

Price based on what the client **receives and can achieve**, not how long it takes:
