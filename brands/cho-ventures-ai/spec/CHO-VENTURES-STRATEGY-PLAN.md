## 1. Market Opportunity: Jacksonville, Florida

Jacksonville represents a Top-10 U.S. growth market with powerful long-term tailwinds:

### Market Demographics
- **Population Growth**: ~53% since 2001 (US Census)
- **Metro Population**: ~1.76M

- **Job Market Ranking**: #2 Hottest Job Market (WSJ 2024)

- **State Income Tax**: 0%
- **Cost of Living**: 15-30% lower vs. Miami, Orlando, Atlanta
- **Fortune 500 HQs**: 4 (FIS, CSX, FNF, Landstar)
