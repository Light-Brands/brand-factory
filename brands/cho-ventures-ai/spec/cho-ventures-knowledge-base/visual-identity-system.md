## Gradient Palette

Dynamic gradient combinations inspired by the Generation Regeneration book cover and brand principles. Use these for headers, hero sections, CTAs, and visual emphasis.

### Regenerative Growth
- **Colors:** Sage Green → Seafoam
- **CSS:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #6EA451 0%, #A6DCCD 100%)`
- **Use for:** Success states, growth metrics, environmental impact

### Calm Authority
- **Colors:** Teal → Seafoam
- **CSS:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #4A6264 0%, #A6DCCD 100%)`
- **Use for:** LinkedIn headers, professional CTAs, trust-building

### Sacred Wisdom
- **Colors:** Purple → Seafoam
- **CSS:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #8B7BA8 0%, #A6DCCD 100%)`
- **Use for:** Book launch content, sacred geometry themes, spiritual regeneration messaging

## Generation Regeneration Book Cover

### Visual Design Analysis

The book cover establishes a powerful visual identity that connects Tony's brand across all entities through its regenerative color palette and sacred geometry.

#### Color Harmony
- Teal to Green gradient (regeneration)
- Blue accents (innovation & trust)
- Earth tones (grounding & authenticity)
- Seamlessly integrates with brand palette

#### Design Elements
- Flower of Life pattern (sacred geometry)
- Globe imagery (global impact)
- Interconnected circles (ecosystems thinking)
- Clean typography (clarity & authority)
