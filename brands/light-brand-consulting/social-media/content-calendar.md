## Ongoing Monthly Rhythm

### Facebook (3x/week)
- **Monday:** Illumination Insight (Educational)
- **Wednesday:** Philosophy / Principle (Brand)
- **Friday:** Transformation Story OR Offer (Social Proof / Conversion) — alternate weeks

### LinkedIn Company Page (2–3x/week)
- **Tuesday:** Thought Leadership (Educational)
- **Thursday:** Case Study OR Methodology (Social Proof / Educational)
- **Saturday (optional):** Service Spotlight or Industry Commentary

### Dan's LinkedIn Personal Profile (3–5x/week)
- **Monday:** Personal insight or observation
- **Tuesday:** Share company post with personal commentary
- **Wednesday:** Industry commentary or hot take
- **Thursday:** Client story or lesson learned (first-person)
- **Friday:** Share article, podcast, or resource with perspective

### LinkedIn Newsletter "The Illumination" (Biweekly)
- Published every other Tuesday
- 800–1,200 words
- One clear insight per issue

### Facebook Live "AI Clarity Hour" (Monthly)
- Last Friday of the month
- 30 minutes, free, open Q&A
- Soft CTA to Illumination Session at end

## Content Repurposing Flow

```
LinkedIn Long-Form Post
    ↓
Facebook Adapted Version (shorter, warmer)
    ↓
LinkedIn Newsletter Deep-Dive (expanded)
    ↓
Facebook Live Topic (interactive)
    ↓
Quote Graphics (Instagram/Stories)
    ↓
Email Newsletter Snippet
```

Every piece of content should be created once and adapted 3–4 times across platforms.

## Voice & Tone Reminders

### We ARE
- Clear, confident, urgent, direct, warm, expert

### We ARE NOT
- Jargon-heavy, arrogant, anxious, aggressive, soft, inaccessible

### Always Use
- Illuminate, reveal, amplify, capacity, transformation, clarity, fullest potential, momentum

### Never Use
- Disrupt, synergy, leverage (as verb), pivot, revolutionary, game-changing, best-in-class, journey, passion

### Platform Tone Adjustments
- **Facebook:** Slightly warmer, more conversational, shorter paragraphs, occasional question to audience
- **LinkedIn:** More professional, data-backed, longer-form acceptable, industry-specific language welcome
- **Dan's Personal LinkedIn:** Most authentic voice — first person, vulnerable where appropriate, opinionated
