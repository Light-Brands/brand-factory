## Design Philosophy

### Core Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Nervous System First** | Every design choice supports regulation and safety |
| **Nature Integration** | Buildings emerge from landscape, not imposed upon it |
| **Sacred Geometry** | Intentional proportions that feel harmonious |
| **Sensory Sanctuary** | Careful attention to sound, light, smell, touch |
| **Privacy + Community** | Easy solitude, easy connection—guest controls |
| **Materiality** | Natural materials that age gracefully |
| **Simplicity** | Clean lines, no clutter, spaciousness |

### Biophilic Design Mandates

1. **Direct Nature Access:** Every room opens to outdoors
2. **Natural Materials:** Wood, stone, earth, natural fibers
3. **Natural Light:** Maximize daylight, minimize artificial
4. **Water Features:** Sound and sight of water throughout
5. **Living Systems:** Plants integrated into architecture
6. **Organic Forms:** Curves and natural shapes over rigid geometry
7. **Views of Nature:** Sight lines to gardens, sky, horizon

### What the Space Must Feel Like

- **Safe** — The body recognizes this as a place to rest
- **Held** — Contained but not confined
- **Quiet** — Acoustic peace, no intrusive sounds
- **Warm** — Not cold, not clinical, genuinely welcoming
- **Sacred** — Reverence built into the bones
- **Beautiful** — Aesthetic nourishment as medicine

## Site Requirements

### Location Criteria

| Criterion | Requirement | Priority |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Land Size** | 0.5-1 hectare minimum | Required |
| **Climate** | Warm, stable, minimal extreme weather | Required |
| **Legal Environment** | Favorable for plant medicine practice | Required |
| **Airport Access** | Within 2-3 hours of international airport | Required |
| **Privacy** | Not visible from public roads, isolated | Required |
| **Natural Beauty** | Inherent landscape value | High |
| **Water Source** | Reliable clean water access | Required |
| **Power Grid** | Reliable electricity or solar potential | Required |
| **Internet** | Fiber or reliable satellite available | Required |
| **Emergency Services** | Hospital within 1 hour | Required |
| **Local Community** | Supportive or neutral local reception | High |

### First Location: Mexico

**Target Regions:**
- Oaxaca (mountain retreats)
- Jalisco (Lake Chapala area)
- Riviera Maya (jungle settings)
- Baja California Sur (desert landscapes)

**Mexico-Specific Considerations:**
- Ejido vs. private land ownership
- Water rights and reliability
- Building permits and regulations
- Local labor and materials
- Security infrastructure

### Topography & Orientation

| Element | Ideal |
|---------|-------|
| **Elevation** | Slight variation for interest, not extreme slopes |
| **Drainage** | Natural water flow away from buildings |
| **Sun Orientation** | Guest rooms with morning light (east-facing) |
| **Prevailing Winds** | Cross-ventilation opportunity |
| **Views** | Natural focal points (mountains, water, sky) |
| **Shade** | Existing mature trees where possible |

### Circulation

- **Primary Path:** Gentle, meandering main pathway connecting all zones
- **Secondary Paths:** Smaller paths for privacy and exploration
- **Covered Walkways:** Protection from rain/sun between major buildings
- **Night Lighting:** Subtle, ground-level path lighting only

## Building Types

### Guest Casitas

**Quantity:** 20-30 units
**Size:** 35-50 m² each
**Occupancy:** Single or couple

**Standard Features:**

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Bedroom** | King or queen bed, natural materials |
| **Bathroom** | En-suite, walk-in shower, natural stone |
| **Private Outdoor** | Covered terrace or deck (10-15 m²) |
| **Views** | Nature views, no neighboring casita visible |
| **Climate Control** | Cross-ventilation + quiet HVAC |
| **Storage** | Ample closet and surface space |
| **Work Surface** | Small desk/table for journaling |
| **Seating** | Comfortable chair and/or daybed |

**Room Categories:**

| Category | Size | Features | Quantity |
|----------|------|----------|----------|
| **Garden** | 35 m² | Standard, garden view | 10-12 |
| **Deluxe** | 40 m² | Larger terrace, premium location | 8-10 |
| **Suite** | 50 m² | Separate living area, best views | 4-6 |
| **Accessible** | 40 m² | Full accessibility features | 2 |

### Ceremony Space

**Main Ceremony Room:**
- Capacity: 15-20 people lying down
- High ceilings (minimum 4m)
- Natural light controllable to complete darkness
- Exceptional acoustics (sound isolated)
- Sacred geometry in proportions
- Natural materials throughout
- Private bathrooms adjacent
- Facilitator/monitoring area
- Emergency equipment discreet but accessible

**Supporting Spaces:**
- Preparation room (gathering before ceremony)
- Integration room (gentle transition space)
- Staff observation area (monitoring without intrusion)
- Clean/sterile area (medical supplies)

### Dining Hall

- Capacity: 40-50 seats
- Long communal tables + smaller tables option
- Open to garden/nature on at least one side
- Visible (or semi-visible) kitchen
- Natural materials, warm lighting
- Acoustic treatment for comfortable conversation
- Adjacent outdoor dining terrace

### Medical Center

| Area | Size | Function |
|------|------|----------|
| **Reception** | 15 m² | Welcome, waiting |
| **Exam Room 1** | 15 m² | General examination |
| **Exam Room 2** | 15 m² | Cardiac/monitoring |
| **Lab Area** | 10 m² | Blood draw, basic testing |
| **Pharmacy** | 10 m² | Medication storage, preparation |
| **Office** | 12 m² | Medical director |
| **Recovery** | 20 m² | Post-procedure recovery |
| **Storage** | 15 m² | Equipment, supplies |

**Medical Equipment:**
- ECG/EKG machine
- Cardiac monitors (portable for ceremony)
- Defibrillator
- Crash cart with emergency medications
- Oxygen supply
- Blood draw and basic lab capability
- Secure medication storage

### Wellness Pavilion

**Bio-Optimization Suite:**

| Modality | Space | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **Infrared Sauna** | 2 units | Private rooms |
| **Traditional Sauna** | 1 unit | 4-6 person |
| **Cold Plunge** | 2 pools | Indoor/outdoor options |
| **Red Light Therapy** | 2 stations | Full-body panels |
| **HBOT** | 2 chambers | Mild or clinical grade |
| **IV Therapy** | 4 chairs | Comfortable lounge setting |
| **Float Tank** | 1-2 tanks | Optional addition |

**Treatment Rooms:**
- 6-10 private treatment rooms (massage, somatic)
- Each room: 15-18 m², natural light controllable
- Dedicated breathwork/group room (40 m²)
- Counseling room (smaller, intimate, 12 m²)

**Movement Studio:**
- 80-100 m² open floor space
- Natural light, garden views
- Sound system
- Yoga props, movement equipment
- Natural flooring (wood or bamboo)

| Zone | Approach |

- Color temperature: 2700-3000K (warm)
- All lights dimmable
- No fluorescent or harsh LED
- Circadian rhythm awareness

## Landscaping & Grounds

### Garden Zones

| Zone | Character | Plants |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Arrival Garden** | Welcoming, transition | Large trees, flowing water |
| **Central Garden** | Community gathering | Mixed plantings, seating |
| **Sacred Garden** | Quiet contemplation | Medicinal plants, sacred geometry |
| **Kitchen Garden** | Productive, educational | Herbs, vegetables, fruit |
| **Native Garden** | Wild, natural | Indigenous species |
| **Water Garden** | Tranquility | Pools, fountains, aquatic plants |

### Hardscape

- **Paths:** Permeable materials, gentle curves
- **Patios:** Natural stone, integrated seating
- **Walls:** Low stone or living walls, not barriers
- **Edges:** Soft transitions, no harsh lines

### Water Features

| Feature | Location | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Entry Fountain** | Arrival garden | Transition, cleansing |
| **Pool** | Central or near wellness | Swimming, cooling |
| **Stream/Channel** | Throughout grounds | Connection, sound |
| **Reflection Pool** | Near ceremony space | Contemplation |
| **Cold Plunge** | Wellness pavilion | Therapy (indoor/outdoor) |

### Pool Design

- Natural pool design (no harsh chlorine) or ozone/salt system
- Organic shape, integrated into landscape
- Varying depths for different uses
- Covered and open sections
- Shower facilities adjacent
- Lounge seating nearby

## Infrastructure

### Power

| System | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Primary** | Grid connection (where reliable) |
| **Solar** | Significant PV installation for supplement |
| **Battery** | Storage for critical systems backup |
| **Generator** | Diesel or propane backup for medical |

**Critical Power Needs:**
- Medical center (uninterruptible)
- Ceremony monitoring equipment
- Refrigeration (food, medications)
- Security systems
- Communications

### Water

| System | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Source** | Well, municipal, or reliable surface water |
| **Treatment** | Filtration, UV, as needed for potability |
| **Hot Water** | Solar thermal + backup heating |
| **Recycling** | Greywater for irrigation |
| **Rainwater** | Collection for non-potable use |

### Waste

| System | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Wastewater** | Septic or treatment wetland |
| **Composting** | Kitchen and garden waste |
| **Recycling** | Comprehensive sorting |
| **Medical Waste** | Proper disposal protocols |

### Communications

| System | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Internet** | Fiber or high-speed satellite |
| **Internal** | WiFi throughout (guest option to disconnect) |
| **Phone** | VoIP + mobile backup |
| **Emergency** | Satellite phone for true emergencies |

### Security

| System | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Perimeter** | Fencing (discrete), controlled access |
| **Entry** | Gatehouse, guest/staff identification |
| **Cameras** | Perimeter only, never in private spaces |
