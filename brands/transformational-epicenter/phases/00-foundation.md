## Decision Points

### Must Decide in Phase 0

1. **Funding model:** Self-funded, angel, institutional, or hybrid?
2. **Mexico region:** Which area offers best combination of factors?
3. **Medical partnership model:** Employed physicians or contract group?
4. **Digital build vs. buy:** Custom development or adapt existing platform?
5. **Brand positioning:** How public, how soon?

---

## Success Criteria for Phase 0

Phase 0 is complete when:

- [ ] Legal entities established (US and Mexico)
- [ ] Medical Director hired and engaged
- [ ] Core team of 5+ assembled
- [ ] Seed funding secured (or self-funded commitment)
- [ ] Mexico site identified and under contract
- [ ] Medical and safety protocols drafted
- [ ] Digital ecosystem in active development
- [ ] Training frameworks outlined
- [ ] Phase 1 plan detailed and approved
