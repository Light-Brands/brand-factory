# Phased Implementation Budget

## Sovereign Consulting Intelligence Platform

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 2026

-----

## Budget Philosophy

This budget is structured around three principles:

1. **Start lean** — Minimize capital until approach is validated
1. **Scale with revenue** — Infrastructure grows with client base
1. **Progressive independence** — Build toward sovereignty incrementally

## Scenario Analysis

### Scenario A: Conservative Path

- Start with Phase 1, evaluate before Phase 2
- Use cloud GPUs for training (no CapEx)
- Hybrid indefinitely (don’t pursue full sovereignty)

**4-Year Investment: $1,200,000 - $2,500,000**

### Scenario B: Moderate Investment

- Complete Phases 1 and 2
- Partial sovereignty (colocation, mid-range GPUs)
- Maintain some API dependency for edge cases

**4-Year Investment: $2,000,000 - $4,500,000**

### Scenario C: Full Sovereignty

- Aggressive timeline
- Premium hardware (H100 cluster)
- Complete independence by end of Year 4

**4-Year Investment: $5,000,000 - $8,500,000**

## Recommendations

### Immediate (Months 1-6)

- Budget: $50,000 - $150,000
- Focus: Build platform, prove concept with existing clients
- Risk: Low

### Near-Term (Months 7-18)

- Budget: $200,000 - $600,000
- Focus: Scale operations, begin training experiments
- Decision Point: Validate model training approach before major investment

### Long-Term (Months 19-48)

- Budget: $1,500,000 - $6,000,000+
- Focus: Full sovereignty based on validated approach
- Contingent: Revenue growth supports investment

### Key Decision Gates

|Gate               |Timing  |Criteria                                   |
|-------------------|--------|-------------------------------------------|
|Proceed to Phase 2?|Month 12|20+ active clients, positive unit economics|
|Invest in training?|Month 15|Successful small-scale experiments         |
|Full sovereignty?  |Month 24|Model quality validated, revenue > $1M/year|

## Appendix: Cost Assumptions

### Cloud Pricing Assumptions

- AWS/GCP standard pricing (no reserved instances)
- 20% buffer for overages
- US regions

### AI API Assumptions

- February 2026 pricing
- No volume discounts applied
- Mixed model usage

### Hardware Assumptions

- NVIDIA H100 at $35,000-$40,000 per GPU
- 3-year depreciation
- 10% annual maintenance
- 6-12 month lead times

### Facility Assumptions

- Tier 3 colocation
- $150-$200 per kW/month
- 100% redundant power
