## Reader Journey

The reader enters the domain of the sacred. Masculine and feminine energies have been weaponized against each other. The ancient feminine technologies of healing, birth, and community are buried. Organized religion serves male dominance. Sacred science is classified or destroyed. Indigenous wisdom — five centuries of accumulated knowledge — is erased or commodified. Jewish culture is persecuted for carrying light. Jesus's message of love is reduced to a symbol of torture. Sports replace cooperation with competition. Individualism replaces community. Family structures are atomized. And elders are separated from the young, severing the chain of wisdom. By the end of Part 7, the reader understands: *The architecture doesn't just exploit the body and mind. It attacks the soul — suppressing everything that connects us to the sacred, to each other, and to who we actually are.*
