# Systemic Domination

## The Architecture of Spiritual Distortion

A dream about seeing clearly — naming the machinery of control operating across human civilization, not as conspiracy, but as observable, interlocking systems that shape beliefs, behaviors, and spiritual perceptions. This "architecture of control" spans nearly every sector of society — from healthcare and food to media, finance, religion, technology, and even language and leisure.

---

## The Through-Line

> What does it look like when every major system in a society slowly bends
> toward serving the same small set of interests — and most people inside
> it can't see the shape of the whole thing?

This dream maps the architecture. Not to despair. To see.
