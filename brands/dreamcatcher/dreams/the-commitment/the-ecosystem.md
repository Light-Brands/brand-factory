## The Thesis

Every piece of software that touches real estate today was built by a different company, for a different part of the process, with no connection to anything else. Property management does not talk to construction management. Lending does not talk to title. Valuation does not talk to insurance. Analytics does not talk to operations.

There are over 50 major software vendors serving the real estate industry. They generated $9.5 billion in revenue in 2024. Combined, the top five are worth $75-85 billion in market capitalization. And none of them were built with AI at the core.

**We replace all of them.**

Not by building one product. By building 15 independent products -- each one best-in-class, each one powered by the same sovereign AI, each one feeding data to every other product. Independent entities that fuel each other. One at a time. Learn and expand.

When all 15 are running, they form something that has never existed: the AGI for real estate. A single intelligence that understands every aspect of the built world -- from how a building is designed, financed, constructed, marketed, leased, managed, maintained, insured, valued, and sold. One intelligence, trained on the full stack of real estate data, getting smarter in every domain because knowledge transfers across domains.

A maintenance pattern in property management improves construction quality prediction. A tenant screening insight improves lending risk models. A valuation model improves insurance underwriting. An energy optimization from one building reduces costs in ten thousand buildings.

No single-product company can compete with this. They would have to build all 15 products and connect them with a unified AI trained on a decade of operational data. By the time they start, we are years ahead.

This is how you own a category permanently.
