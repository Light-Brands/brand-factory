## The Thesis

Every time someone buys, sells, owns, or operates a property, they interact with a dozen broken industries. Mortgage lenders who take 45 days to close. Insurance companies that deny claims as a business model. Title companies that charge $2,500 for a 5% risk product. Moving companies that hold furniture hostage. Home warranty firms with 21,000 BBB complaints. Property tax offices that overassess 30-60% of homes and count on nobody appealing.

These industries are not just bad at what they do. They are structurally designed to extract maximum value while delivering minimum service. They exist because consumers have no alternative. No one has ever connected them. No one has ever rebuilt them with AI from the ground up. No one has ever offered them all in one place.

Until now.

We build one app. One account. One profile for you and your property. Use it once and we know everything -- your property, your preferences, your financial picture, your service history. Every service you need for the entire lifecycle of property ownership, offered seamlessly, priced fairly, powered by AI, and delivered by a lean team of domain experts across a few key verticals.

We do not partner with the broken incumbents. We replace them. Every service we offer, we own. Every dollar of margin, we capture. Every interaction, we learn from. The data from lending improves insurance underwriting. The data from insurance improves property valuation. The data from property management improves lending decisions. The flywheel never stops.

This is the Tesla model applied to real estate. Tesla did not build a better car and hand it to a dealer network. Tesla built the car, the charging network, the insurance product, the financing, the service centers, and the software. Every touchpoint. Every revenue stream. Every customer relationship. Owned.

We do the same for real estate.

That is our book of business from day one. We know those buildings better than any insurance company ever could -- because we built them, we manage them, we maintain them.

Then we open to the market. Every building on our Manage Engine gets access to insurance priced on real data, not guesses. Buildings that maintain well pay less. Buildings with our Energy Engine monitoring pay less. Buildings with our security monitoring pay less. Everything connects.

**Instant Title.** AI-powered title search that runs in minutes, not weeks. Direct-to-consumer. No agent network. No referral fees. Digital closing with Remote Online Notarization (RON).

| Feature | Legacy Title Company | Our Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Title search | 3-10 business days, human abstractor | AI search of digitized records, minutes to hours |
| Premium | $1,000-$2,500 | $300-$800 (same coverage, direct pricing) |
| Closing process | In-person, attorney-required in 21 states | RON-enabled digital closing (legal in 40+ states) |
| Claims risk | 5.1% loss ratio | Same or lower (AI catches more issues upfront) |
| Customer selection | Agent/lender selects for you | You select. We disclose pricing. Full transparency. |

### Revenue Model

Title insurance premiums (50-70% cheaper than legacy but with 90%+ gross margin due to eliminated distribution costs) + closing service fees.

At 10% market penetration: $500M-$1B in revenue at 70%+ margin.

## The Regulatory Path

### Licenses Required

| Service | License Required | Difficulty | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Home Services | General business license only | Low | Immediate |
| Property Tax | No special license (contingency consulting) | Low | Immediate |
| Utilities | No special license (data platform) | Low | Immediate |
| Home Warranty | Service contract provider license (varies by state) | Medium | 6-12 months |
| Security | Alarm company license (per state) | Medium | 3-6 months per state |
| Design/Furnishing | None | Low | Immediate |
| Moving | FMCSA broker license | Medium | 3-6 months |
| Title Insurance | Title insurance agent/underwriter license (per state) | High | 12-24 months |
| Mortgage Lending | NMLS licensing + state licenses | High | 12-18 months |
| Insurance | MGA license (per state), eventual carrier license | Very High | 18-36 months |

### Strategy: Start Where Licenses Are Not Required

Six of our ten services require no special regulatory approval. We launch those first, build the customer base and data foundation, then use that position to launch the licensed financial products with a permanent data advantage.
