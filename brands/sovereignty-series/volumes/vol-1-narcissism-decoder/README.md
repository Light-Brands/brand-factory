## Key Frameworks

### The 3-3-3 Rule
**3 Seconds:** Pause before responding
**3 Breaths:** Inhale 4, exhale 6 to engage parasympathetic nervous system
**3 Words (or fewer):** Keep responses short to limit ammunition

### The Decoder Cards System
- **Core (19 cards):** Gaslighting, triangulation, love-bombing, shame-dumping, etc.
- **Advanced (18 cards):** Sophisticated tactics during high-stakes moments
- **Emergency:** For crisis situations requiring immediate clarity

### Reading Paths
- **Crisis Path:** Chapter 18 → Chapter 2 → Chapter 17 → Chapter 11
- **Understanding Path:** Read in order
- **Healing Path:** Chapter 17 → Chapter 19 → Chapter 10 → Chapter 4
