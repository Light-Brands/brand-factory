### 2.2 Reserve Management Policy (NEW - MANDATORY)

#### Reserve Requirements

| Reserve Type | Requirement | Purpose |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| **Operating Reserve** | 6 months operating costs | Ensure TWGE continuity |
| **Liquidity Reserve** | 3-month rolling redemption coverage | Vendor redemption capacity |
| **Emergency Reserve** | 10% of total capital raised | Black swan protection |

#### Reserve Calculation

```
Liquidity Reserve Requirement = (Average Monthly Redemptions × 3) × 1.2 safety factor

Example:
- Monthly redemptions: $50,000
- Required reserve: $50,000 × 3 × 1.2 = $180,000 minimum
```

#### Reserve Triggers

| Trigger Level | Reserve % | Action Required |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Green** | >150% of requirement | Normal operations |
| **Yellow** | 100-150% | Increase monitoring, reduce issuance caps |
| **Orange** | 75-100% | Pause new enrollments, limit redemptions to 7-day cycles |
| **Red** | <75% | Emergency protocol: 14-day redemption cycles, halt new credits |

#### Reserve Governance

- Monthly reserve reports to Advisory Council
- Quarterly external audit of reserve adequacy
- Annual stress test simulating 3x redemption demand
- Reserve policy changes require 2/3 Advisory Council approval
