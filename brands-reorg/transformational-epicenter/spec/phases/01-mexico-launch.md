# Phase 1: Mexico Launch

> *The first Epicenter opens its doors.*

**Status:** Planning
**Timeline:** Year 1-3
**Focus:** Build, staff, launch, refine the first location

---

## Phase Objectives

1. Design and construct the Mexico facility
2. Hire and train full operating team
3. Establish all operational systems
4. Launch digital ecosystem
5. Welcome first guests
6. Refine all systems based on experience
7. Achieve operational excellence

---

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Construction delays | High | Medium | Buffer in timeline, penalty clauses |
| Permit issues | Medium | High | Local legal counsel, relationships |
| Staff recruitment | Medium | High | Start early, competitive packages |
| Cost overruns | High | Medium | Contingency budget, value engineering |
| Slow initial demand | Medium | Medium | Soft launch, PR strategy, patience |
| Quality issues | Medium | High | Training, dry runs, continuous QA |

---

## Success Criteria for Phase 1

Phase 1 is complete when:

- [ ] Facility fully constructed and operational
- [ ] Full team hired, trained, and performing
- [ ] All programs running successfully
- [ ] Digital ecosystem supporting guest journey
- [ ] 6 months of full operations completed
- [ ] Guest satisfaction >4.8/5.0
- [ ] Safety record: Zero serious incidents
- [ ] Financial: Path to unit profitability clear
- [ ] Learnings documented for replication
