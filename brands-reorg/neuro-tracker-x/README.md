## Brand Overview

NeuroTrackerX is a cognitive training system backed by 20+ years of neuroscience research, 120+ published papers, and 50+ patents. The platform helps users enhance brain performance through scientifically-validated 3D multiple object tracking exercises.

### Core Value Proposition
- 6-minute adaptive training sessions
- 16+ cognitive domains improved
- Multi-platform access (tablets, laptops, TVs)
- Dual-tasking options for elite performance

### Target Markets
- **Sports:** Professional athletes, teams, performance centers
- **Medical:** Concussion recovery, dementia support, neuropsychology
- **Wellness:** ADHD, stress management, cognitive aging
- **Enterprise:** Military, aviation, corporate performance (1,000+ businesses)

### Scientific Foundation
- 120+ published research papers
- 100+ research institutes
- 50+ patents issued/pending
- 20 years of neuroscience research
